About

Dickie Lakeshore is the stage name of Richard Gibbs, a stand-up comedian based in the Niagara region of Ontario.

You’ll find him on the local circuit — Legions, comedy clubs like Spotlight and Yuk Yuk’s Niagara, fundraisers, and alongside friends from the Talkin’ Funny podcast community.

He’s appeared on Fort Erie Radio and helped raise money for local journalism and youth theatre — because a good laugh and a good cause go well together.

“When people are laughing and having a beverage, they’re generous and they’re happy.”
